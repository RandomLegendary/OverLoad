Welcome To Overload!

Rules:

Click = Explosion:

Every click on a reactor does two things:

Reduces its own value by 1.

Forces all adjacent reactors (up/down/left/right) to also decrease by 1.

One exception: This happens every time, except when the clicked reactor is 0.

Chain Reactions:

If a neighbor reaches 0 from the explosion, it does NOT trigger additional explosions (unless clicked).

(Clarification: Unlike classic Minesweeper, explosions only happen on your clicks, not automatically when a reactor hits 0.)

Goal:

Reduce every reactor to exactly 0 in the fewest clicks possible.
